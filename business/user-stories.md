# User Stories
## Epic: Idea Generation
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to input market trends and keywords, so that I can generate a list of potential micro-SaaS ideas.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to input market trends and keywords.
		- The system generates a list of at least 10 potential micro-SaaS ideas based on the input.
		- The list of ideas is prioritized based on market demand and potential revenue.
	+ Estimated Complexity: M
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to browse a library of pre-existing micro-SaaS ideas, so that I can get inspiration for my own projects.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system provides a library of at least 50 pre-existing micro-SaaS ideas.
		- The library is categorized by market trend and keyword.
		- Users can filter and sort the library by various criteria (e.g. revenue potential, market demand).
	+ Estimated Complexity: L
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to receive alerts for new and emerging market trends, so that I can stay ahead of the competition.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system provides users with regular alerts for new and emerging market trends.
		- The alerts are personalized based on the user's interests and previous searches.
		- The system allows users to customize the frequency and type of alerts they receive.
	+ Estimated Complexity: M

## Epic: Idea Validation
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to input my micro-SaaS idea and receive a validation score, so that I can determine its potential for success.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to input their micro-SaaS idea and receive a validation score.
		- The validation score is based on market demand, competition, and potential revenue.
		- The system provides users with a detailed report explaining the validation score.
	+ Estimated Complexity: L
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to compare my micro-SaaS idea to similar existing products, so that I can identify areas for differentiation.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to compare their micro-SaaS idea to similar existing products.
		- The comparison includes metrics such as market demand, competition, and revenue potential.
		- The system provides users with recommendations for differentiating their product.
	+ Estimated Complexity: M
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to receive feedback from potential customers on my micro-SaaS idea, so that I can validate its potential for success.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to share their micro-SaaS idea with potential customers and receive feedback.
		- The feedback is collected through surveys or other feedback mechanisms.
		- The system provides users with a summary of the feedback and recommendations for improvement.
	+ Estimated Complexity: L

## Epic: Idea Prioritization
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to prioritize my micro-SaaS ideas based on their potential for success, so that I can focus on the most promising projects.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to prioritize their micro-SaaS ideas based on their validation scores.
		- The system provides users with a ranked list of their ideas, with the most promising projects first.
		- The system allows users to filter and sort their ideas by various criteria (e.g. market demand, revenue potential).
	+ Estimated Complexity: S
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to track the performance of my micro-SaaS products over time, so that I can identify areas for improvement.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to track the performance of their micro-SaaS products over time.
		- The system provides users with metrics such as revenue, customer acquisition, and retention.
		- The system allows users to set goals and receive alerts when their products are not meeting their targets.
	+ Estimated Complexity: M

## Epic: Integration and Workflow
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to integrate ideation-validate with my existing workflow tools, so that I can streamline my product development process.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system provides APIs and integrations with popular workflow tools (e.g. Trello, Asana, Jira).
		- The system allows users to automate tasks and workflows based on their micro-SaaS ideas and validation scores.
		- The system provides users with a dashboard for tracking their ideas and workflows.
	+ Estimated Complexity: L
* As a micro-SaaS creator, I want to collaborate with team members on my micro-SaaS ideas, so that I can get feedback and input from others.
	+ Acceptance Criteria:
		- The system allows users to invite team members to collaborate on their micro-SaaS ideas.
		- The system provides real-time commenting and feedback mechanisms.
		- The system allows users to assign tasks and track progress.
	+ Estimated Complexity: M
