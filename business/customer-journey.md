# customer-journey.md

| Phase | Trigger Event | Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Key Metric |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|---------------|---------------------------|------------|
| **Aware** | • Seeing a LinkedIn post or Reddit thread about “micro‑SaaS ideas” <br>• SEO hit on “AI idea validation” <br>• Referral from a peer | • Low brand visibility in niche channels <br>• Competing with generic idea‑generation tools | Curious, skeptical | • Publish a 5‑minute explainer video that shows a live demo of the AI engine <br>• Offer a “micro‑SaaS idea of the week” newsletter (free) <br>• Leverage micro‑influencers in the SaaS founder community | **Awareness Rate** – % of traffic from niche sources (target: 25% of total visits) |
| **Consider** | • Landing on the product page after a search <br>• Clicking “See AI Demo” | • Overwhelming technical jargon <br>• Unclear ROI metrics <br>• No social proof | Interested, cautious | • Interactive “Idea Validation Calculator” that instantly shows projected TAM, CAC, and payback <br>• Embed case studies of 3 validated micro‑SaaS launches <br>• Live chat with a product strategist (30‑sec response) | **Consider‑to‑Trial Conversion** – % of visitors who sign up for a free trial (target: 12%) |
| **Try** | • Signing up for a 14‑day free trial | • Complex onboarding steps <br>• Limited data sources for validation <br>• No guidance on how to interpret results | Excited, overwhelmed | • 3‑step onboarding wizard: 1) Upload niche keywords 2) AI generates 5 idea briefs 3) Validate each with market data <br>• In‑app “Idea Scorecard” with actionable insights <br>• Prompt to schedule a 15‑min “Idea‑Validation Call” with a consultant | **Trial Activation Rate** – % of trial users who complete the 3‑step wizard (target: 65%) |
| **Adopt** | • Receiving a high‑score idea that aligns with personal expertise | • Fear of investing time in a low‑probability idea <br>• Unclear pricing tiers | Confident, hopeful | • Offer a “Launch Checklist” PDF that maps the next 30 days <br>• Provide a community forum for micro‑SaaS founders <br>• Early‑bird discount for the first 100 paying customers | **Activation Rate** – % of trial users who upgrade to a paid plan (target: 30%) |
| **Expand** | • Successfully launching a micro‑SaaS and seeing traction | • Need for advanced analytics <br>• Limited integration with other tools | Proud, ambitious | • Tiered add‑ons: 1) Advanced market‑trend analytics 2) Automated competitor monitoring 3) API access for custom dashboards <br>• Invite to an exclusive “Founder Growth Summit” <br>• Referral program: $50 credit per successful invite | **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** – target ≥ +50 <br>**Referral Rate** – % of paying users who refer ≥ 1 new customer (target: 20%) |

---

### Quick Takeaways

- **Awareness**: Focus on niche content and micro‑influencers; aim for 25 % of traffic from targeted sources.
- **Consider**: Reduce friction by simplifying language and providing instant ROI previews; hit a 12 % conversion to trial.
- **Try**: Keep onboarding to 3 steps; aim for 65 % activation of the trial process.
- **Adopt**: Leverage early‑bird pricing and community support; target 30 % upgrade rate.
- **Expand**: Offer add‑ons and a referral program; aim for NPS ≥ +50 and 20 % referral rate.

These metrics and delight points will guide the next sprint of product improvements and marketing focus.
